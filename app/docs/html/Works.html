Leap year algorithm

An algorithm to determine if it is a leap year, adapted from the logic shown in this wikipedia page: Accessed September, 2016 https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leap_year#Algorithm

Day of the week algorithm

An algorithm to determine the day of the week of the first day of the year, adapted from the algortihm on the "disparate variation" of Gauss's algorithm, shown here: Accessed September, 2016 https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leap_year#Algorithm

Regular Expressions

Regular expressions were built for date parsing using a tutorial written by Jan Goyvaerts. Accessed September 2016 https://www.regular-expressions.info/dates.html

Flask Extensions & Best Practices

We consulted the official Flask documentation while writing our server, and adapted many of the examples to fit our needs. Since much of what we used is simply The Way to implement a given feature, we did not cite them individually within the code.

Flask Documentation by Armin Ronacher Accessed September 2016 http://flask.pocoo.org/docs/0.11/

Flask Micro-Blog Tutorial

This extensive tutorial was used as a model for our authentication, and was also where we got the Bootstrap code for our client-side CSS.

The Flask Mega-Tutorial by Miguel Grinberg Accessed September 2016 http://blog.miguelgrinberg.com/post/the-flask-mega-tutorial-part-i-hello-world
