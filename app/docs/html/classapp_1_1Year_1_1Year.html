Detailed Description

Generates a year, including the months, weeks, and days contained with in it.

Author
Grant Jurgensen

Generates a full year (January 1st through December 31), and auto-fills itself with the months and days within it. It can "link" with other years, so that the weeks within its months are able to bleed over between the year boundaries.

Initializes a Year object.

Parameters
[in]nameAn integer value corresponding to the year, e.g. 2016
[out]returnThe initialized Year object

Initializes a Year object. Calculates whether it is a leap year, and adjusts the months lengths if so. Calculates the weekday of the first day of the year in order to have a reference point for all future days created. Iterates through 12 months, creates each month and fills it with the appropriate days.
